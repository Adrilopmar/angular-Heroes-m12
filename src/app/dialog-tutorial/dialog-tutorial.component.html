Welcome to Angular Heroes Battle Alfa version

Angular Heroes Battle it's a quite simple card game since its an Alfa version. Not even all its mechanics are working yet.

First of all let's take a look at the cards.

  • As you can see, we have got 3 stats:
    • Strenght or Attack
    • Resistance or Defense
    • Help
  • Strenght / Attack: This stat is used when the card is attacking. if the Attack of the attacking card is equal or hihger than defense's defending card, the attacking card will win the battle and score a point for its player.
  • Resistance / Defense: this stat is used when your card is defending. if the defense is hihger than the Strenght of the attacking card, this one will successfully defend and will attack back. Then if the defender card's Strenght is equal of higher than attacker card's defense, this one will score a point for its player.
  • Help: this stat is not used and has no relevance in the game yet. But if you want to know what is it and how it will affect to the battles, keep reading. The help is a boost to the Strenght and defense. It will be triggered when a player lose a battle. So, let's say that Player A attacks to Player B with Celeritas (where its help is 1). Player B defenses successfully and wins the battle point. This way, next card that player A plays will get a boost of X where X is based on the help of the card that has lost the last battle, in this case the boost will be +1 to Strenght and +1 to defense as the help of the last defeateed card's player (player A) is 1.

    • Now let's talk about the turns and the basic rules

  • Each player will have 4 turns. 2 attaking turns and 2 defending turns.
  • Player 1 attacks first. After player 1 has chosen the card that will attack, it is player 2 defending turn and must choose a defending card. Once the battle has ended, and the battle point is earned by the winner, it is player 2 attacking turn. So Player one will defend this time. so turns will be:
    • Turn 1: player 1 attacks - player 2 defends
    • Turn 2: player 2 attakcs - player 1 defends
    • Turn 3: player 1 attacks - player 2 defends
    • Turn 4: player 2 attakcs - player 1 defends
  • Be careful! Doesn't matter if you are attacking or defending, cards can only be played once!

So those are the basics of Angular Heroes Battle game. The game finishes after the 4th turn and the player with most points (whose won most of the battle) will be the winner
